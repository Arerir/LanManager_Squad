## Implementation Details

- **Hub URL:** `{ApiBaseUrl}/hubs/attendance`
- **Auth:** JWT via `options.AccessTokenProvider`
- **Service pattern:** Singleton with transient ViewModel (reconnect on page load)
- **Event handlers:**
  - `UserCheckedIn` → green "✓ You're checked in!" (4s)
  - `UserDoorScanned` direction=Exit → blue "→ You went outside" (4s)
  - `UserDoorScanned` direction=Entry → blue "← Welcome back!" (4s)
- **Filtering:** Both eventId AND userId to avoid cross-user notifications
